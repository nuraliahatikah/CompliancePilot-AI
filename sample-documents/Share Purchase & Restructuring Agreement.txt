SHARE PURCHASE AND ACQUISITION AGREEMENT

This Share Purchase and Acquisition Agreement ("Agreement") is executed between the Transferor and the Transferee.

1. TRANSACTION AND DISPOSAL SCOPE
The Transferor agrees to sell, transfer, and assign unlisted ordinary shares of the target entity incorporated in Malaysia to the Transferee. Both parties acknowledge that this transaction constitutes a formal disposal of a capital asset under Section 4(aa) of the Income Tax Act 1967 and is fully subject to the statutory provisions of the Malaysian Capital Gains Tax (CGT) framework.

2. TREATMENT OF TAXABLE CESSATION EVENTS
Pursuant to Section 65C of the Income Tax Act 1967, any subsequent structural changes required to complete this corporate restructuring—including any associated share redemptions, equity class conversions, or the deliberate extinguishing of equity rights via localized entity liquidation—shall be legally classified as ownership cessation events. Each discrete transaction must be audited individually for potential corporate tax exposure.

3. MANDATORY ELECTRONIC LHDN TAX FILING
In strict compliance with Section 86A of the Income Tax Act 1967, the Transferor covenants to independently prepare and submit the Capital Gains Tax Return Form (Form e-CKM) electronically through the Lembaga Hasil Dalam Negeri (LHDN) MyTax Portal. This tax filing, along with the remittance of the required tax payment (calculated at the statutory rate of 10% on net chargeable gains), must be finalized and cleared strictly within sixty (60) days from the formal execution date of this written agreement.

4. INDEMNIFICATION AND EVIDENCE OF TAX COMPLIANCE
The Transferor shall fully indemnify and hold the Transferee harmless against any financial assessments, penalties, or late compounding interest levied by the LHDN Director General due to late filing or delinquent tax remittance. Within seventy (70) days of execution, the Transferor must provide the Transferee with official system-generated receipts proving successful e-CKM submission and total tax payment clearance.
