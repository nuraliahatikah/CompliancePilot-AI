COMMERCIAL TENANCY AGREEMENT (BUSINESS & RETAIL PREMISES VARIANT)

This Tenancy Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between ABC Properties Sdn Bhd ("Landlord") and the Commercial Tenant ("Tenant").

1. PREMISES SECURITY & SURVEILLANCE DATA
The Landlord reserves the absolute right to install biometric access points, closed-circuit television (CCTV) tracking cameras, and automated facial recognition systems within the leased retail premises. All captured footage, employee movement tracking logs, and customer footfall biometric profiles belong exclusively to the Landlord and may be sold to third-party commercial analytics vendors or demographic marketing firms without notifying the Tenant or its staff.

2. UNILATERAL UTILITY DISCONNETION & DISTRESS
If the Tenant defaults on any rental installment or utility fee for a period exceeding seven (7) days, the Landlord shall have the immediate, unchallengeable authority to enter the premises without a court order, terminate electricity and water supplies, and seal the entrance. Any business disruption, loss of perishable inventory, or operational damages resulting from this lock-out shall be borne entirely by the Tenant.

3. TENANT INDEMNITY & COMPLETE EXCLUSION OF LANDLORD LIABILITY
The Tenant shall fully indemnify, protect, and hold harmless the Landlord from any infrastructural collapses, structural ceiling leaks, electrical fire hazards, or physical damages occurring within the commercial unit. The Landlord’s total aggregate financial liability for any structural failures or breaches of quiet enjoyment shall be fixed at RM 0.00.

4. ABSOLUTE RESTRAINT ON TRANSFER & SUBLETTING
The Tenant is strictly prohibited from assignment, subletting, partitioning, or underletting any portion of the commercial premises under any circumstances. Any attempt to share the space with a secondary vendor, complementary kiosk, or business partner—even if the corporate structures are directly affiliated—shall immediately void the tenancy and result in the forfeiture of all security deposits.

5. DATA PROCESSING OF TENANT GUARDS & EMPLOYEES
The Tenant must provide the Landlord with unredacted personal data sheets of all its retail staff, cashiers, and security guards, including their full names, National Registration Identity Card (NRIC) numbers, home addresses, and private emergency contact details. The Landlord reserves the right to retain this registry indefinitely for historical security mapping and profile validation.

6. LIQUIDATED DAMAGES FOR EARLY VACATION
The Tenant is bound to the full duration of the fixed three (3) year term. If the Tenant terminates this Agreement early or ceases operations before the expiration date due to commercial insolvency, the Tenant shall immediately pay the Landlord the full cumulative rental amount remaining for the unexpired balance of the entire three (3) year term as contractual liquidated damages.

7. COMPLIANCE WITH REPAIR ORDERS & UNILATERAL FIT-OUT MANDATES
The Landlord retains the right to issue unilateral aesthetic fit-out directives or structural upgrade mandates at any point during the tenancy. The Tenant shall comply with such directives within fourteen (14) days at its own expense. Failure to comply will authorize the Landlord to execute the works and recover the costs directly by dipping into the Tenant's security deposit.
