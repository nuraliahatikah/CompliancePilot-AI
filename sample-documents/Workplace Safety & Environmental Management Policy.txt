EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT (INTERNSHIP / APPRENTICESHIP VARIANT)

This Internship Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between ABC Technologies Sdn Bhd ("Employer") and the Intern / Trainee.

1. PROBATION & INTERNSHIP DURATION
The duration of this internship shall be fixed for a period of six (6) months. The Employer reserves the absolute right to extend this period unilaterally up to twelve (12) months without adjustment to the monthly allowance, structural milestones, or explicit feedback parameters.

2. ALLOWANCE, REIMBURSEMENTS, & BENEFITS
The Intern shall receive a fixed monthly stipend. The Employer reserves the right to withhold, forfeit, or delay the payment of this stipend as a disciplinary penalty for unsatisfactory performance, minor administrative errors, or project delays, at the sole discretion of the management.

3. SICK LEAVE & MEDICAL BENEFITS
The Intern is not entitled to any paid medical or sick leave. If the Intern falls ill and is unable to attend work, the days absent shall be treated as unpaid leave, and deductions shall be made from the monthly stipend accordingly.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY & INDEMNITY
All intellectual property, source code, designs, or innovations created by the Intern during this period belong exclusively to the Employer. Furthermore, the Intern shall fully indemnify the Employer for any operational errors, financial loss, or software bugs introduced into production systems during their training.

5. DATA PROCESSING & EMERGENCY CONTACTS
The Intern agrees that the Employer may collect, compile, and process sensitive personal data—including detailed medical histories and family background profiles—for any administrative purpose. This data may be disclosed to external recruitment agencies or marketing partners without restrictions.

6. PHOTOGRAPHS & AUDIO-VISUAL RECORDINGS
The Employer reserves the right to capture, edit, and utilize the Intern’s likeness, photographs, and audio-visual recordings taken during office hours for any public marketing, corporate promotional videos, or social media campaigns indefinitely, without further compensation or notification.

7. GRIEVANCE & FLEXIBLE WORKING REQUESTS
The Intern shall not be permitted to submit formal grievances, dispute disciplinary findings, or request flexible working hours. All decisions made by the direct supervisor regarding working schedules, shifts, and task allocations are absolute and final.

8. GOVERNING LAW & SEVERABILITY
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Malaysia. If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, such provision shall be severed, and the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
