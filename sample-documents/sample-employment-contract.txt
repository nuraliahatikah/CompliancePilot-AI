EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT

This Employment Contract ("Agreement") is entered into between ABC Technologies Sdn Bhd ("Employer") and the Employee.

1. WORKING HOURS
The Employee agrees to work up to ten (10) hours per day, Monday through Saturday, without additional overtime compensation beyond the standard hourly rate.

2. TERMINATION
The Employer may terminate this Agreement at any time without prior notice or payment in lieu of notice, at its sole discretion.

3. WAGE DEDUCTIONS
The Employer reserves the right to deduct wages for any damages, losses, or expenses incurred by the Employee, including training costs and equipment replacement.

4. NON-COMPETE
For a period of five (5) years following termination, the Employee shall not engage in any competing business worldwide, including any technology-related activities.

5. PERSONAL DATA
The Employee consents to the Employer collecting, processing, and sharing personal data with third-party affiliates and partners without prior notification. Personal data may be transferred outside Malaysia for processing and storage.

6. DATA RETENTION
All personal data collected during employment shall be retained indefinitely for the Employer's records and future reference.

7. ANNUAL LEAVE
The Employee is entitled to five (5) days of annual leave per year, inclusive of public holidays.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
All company information shall remain confidential indefinitely after termination of employment.

Signed:
Employer: ABC Technologies Sdn Bhd
Date: 1 January 2025
